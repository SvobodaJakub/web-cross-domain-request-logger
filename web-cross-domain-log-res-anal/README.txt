The .py scripts allow analysis of logs done by web-cross-domain-req-logger.

The .txt files are my logs and blacklists. I am in the Czech Republic which makes it tend more towards .cz domains. I more or less selected random domains to be scanned, some of them from top N lists. 

The blacklist will block many useful features on webpages and will break many webpages.

You are advised to scan domains of your choice yourself and assemble your own blacklist based on your opinions and goals.
